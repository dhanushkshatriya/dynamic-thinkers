Empowering Farmers with AI

At AgroDetect AI, we believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide farmers and agricultural professionals with a powerful tool to detect plant diseases early, saving crops and reducing losses.

Our deep learning model has been trained on thousands of images across 50+ diseases, achieving over 95% accuracy in lab conditions. We continuously update our dataset to include new diseases and crops.

Founded in 2025 by a team of AI researchers and agronomists, we are committed to sustainable agriculture through innovation.
